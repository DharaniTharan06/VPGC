Analysis: The Strategy of Server Consolidation

✅ Answer: This is the optimizer working correctly!

  • It prioritized the two most energy-efficient nodes (node_1 and node_4)

  • This is a known industry strategy called Server Consolidation
  • It saves significant costs by allowing idle servers to enter low-power state
